## 3. What It Is NOT

**NOT a SaaS product.** There is no shared infrastructure. No feature roadmap you do not control. No "we will add that in Q3." Your system is yours. You own the workflows, the configurations, the data, everything.

**NOT a generic "AI automation" service.** Every agent, every workflow, every prompt is built specifically for staffing and recruiting. Built by people who understand that "time-to-fill" is not an abstract metric -- it is the difference between winning and losing a fee.

**NOT a recruiter replacement.** The factory makes your recruiters 2x more effective. It handles the 60-70% of their day consumed by admin tasks -- resume screening, data entry, scheduling, follow-up -- so they can spend time on what humans do best: building relationships, closing deals, and exercising judgment.

**NOT off-the-shelf software.** Every deployment is configured for your ATS, your workflows, your pain points. A light industrial agency filling 200 temp roles a week has fundamentally different needs than an executive search firm placing 3 C-suite candidates a quarter. The factory handles both.

**NOT a consulting engagement.** We do not advise. We do not produce reports. We do not run workshops and leave you with a binder. We BUILD and DEPLOY working automation that runs in production from Day 1.
