# Episode Format and Production Guide

## Interview Episodes (70% of episodes)

- **Target Length:** 35-45 minutes
- **Structure:**
  - [0:00-2:00] **Cold Open** — Start with a punchy stat or quote from later in the conversation. No intro music essay. Hook them in 10 seconds.
  - [2:00-5:00] **The Recruiter's Edge Intro** — Justin frames today's topic and why it matters to every recruiter listening right now. Reference a specific data point from the 67-problem map.
  - [5:00-8:00] **Guest Origin Story** — "Tell me the moment you realized AI was going to change staffing." Let them tell their story. Don't interrupt.
  - [8:00-25:00] **The Deep Dive** — The specific problem, how the guest's agency handles it, where they've tried AI, what worked, what didn't. This is the meat. Get specific — what ATS, how many recruiters, what metrics.
  - [25:00-35:00] **The Playbook** — Concrete, specific takeaways. "If someone listening runs a [agency type] with [X] recruiters, what should they do Monday morning?" Force specificity.
  - [35:00-40:00] **The Crystal Ball** — "Where does [this niche] go in 12-24 months?" Future-looking but grounded in current trajectory.
  - [40:00-42:00] **The One Thing** — "If a listener does ONE thing after this episode, what should it be?" Distill the entire conversation into one action.
  - [42:00-45:00] **Close** — Where to find the guest, what's coming next episode, reminder that show notes link to the open-source repo with the full deep dive for their agency type.

## Solo Deep Dive Episodes (30% of episodes)

- **Target Length:** 20-30 minutes
- **Structure:**
  - [0:00-2:00] **Cold Open** — Start with a specific agency scenario. "Imagine you're running a 15-person contingency agency. It's Monday morning. You have 47 open reqs..."
  - [2:00-5:00] **The Problem** — Frame it with data from the problem map. Real numbers, real impact.
  - [5:00-20:00] **The Solution** — Step by step, tool by tool. Show exactly how AI solves this. Reference specific agents, specific workflows. This is a live walkthrough, not theory.
  - [20:00-25:00] **Real Results** — Case study data. Before and after. "We deployed this for a [agency type] and within 30 days..."
  - [25:00-28:00] **The Action Plan** — What to do Monday morning. Make it specific enough that someone can start without us.
  - [28:00-30:00] **Close** — Show notes, repo link, next episode preview.

## Recurring Segments (Rotate In)

- **"The 67 Problems"** — Spotlight on one specific pain point from the problem map. 3-5 minutes. Data, impact, solution sketch. Build a library over time.
- **"Agent of the Week"** — Highlight one of the 10 AI agents. How it works, what it costs, who it's for. Demo-style narration.
- **"ATS Breakdown"** — Specific tip for Bullhorn, Lever, Greenhouse, etc. "Did you know Bullhorn's API supports [X] but 90% of agencies never use it?"
- **"The Numbers"** — One industry stat that tells a story. 30-60 seconds. "81% of recruiters report burnout. Let that sink in."
- **"Listener Questions"** — Answer questions from the audience. Build community.

## Production Notes

- Record video (studio or remote) for YouTube
- Audio extracted for Spotify/Apple
- Show notes published as markdown in the GitHub repo (show-notes/ directory)
- Each episode tagged with: agency types covered, pain points addressed, agents referenced
- Social clips cut within 24 hours of recording
